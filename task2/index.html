Ratan Naval Tata

A Visionary Leader & Philanthropist

About

Ratan Naval Tata, born on December 28, 1937, is one of India’s most respected industrialists and the former chairman of Tata Sons. Under his leadership, the Tata Group expanded globally, acquiring companies like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley Tea. Despite his immense success, he is known for his humility, ethics, and concern for social welfare.

His philanthropic efforts through the Tata Trusts have impacted millions of lives across India — focusing on education, healthcare, and rural development. He has always believed in integrity over profit and compassion over competition.
